## About  

Creative Coding Utrecht and TivoliVredenburg present an evening featuring James Holden, Turbulente & Eloi el Bon Noi, and Sabrina Verhage & Lucretio, where live electronics and improvisation come together. From layered melodies and IDM influences to minimalist rawness and live visuals, the artists demonstrate how digital tools enhance their artistry.
