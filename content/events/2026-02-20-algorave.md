## About

This Algorave is organised together with the volunteers from the [Vondelbunker in Amsterdam](https://radar.squat.net/nl/amsterdam/vondelbunker). An evening full of live coding music and visuals by artists from our community. There will also be a performance by [Nabír](https://www.instagram.com/nabir___/), a live coder from Rome, Italy.
