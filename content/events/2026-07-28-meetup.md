## About  
💻✨ Live Coding Meet-Up at BlaBlaLab ✨💻

Got a work-in-progress, a new tool to share, or an idea you'd like to explore with fellow makers?

Join us for an evening dedicated to live coding, creative experimentation, and community. Whether you're building performances, exploring live visuals, writing algorithms, or taking your very first steps into coding, this meet-up offers a welcoming space to learn, share, and collaborate.

Led by Eindhoven-based artist Wilbert Vogel @lil_bleep.xyz , each session combines feedback, technical support, creative exchange, and collective brainstorming around a community-driven project.

🕡 18:30 Walk-in, food & drinks
🖥️ 19:00 Share Your Screen! Present your work, experiments, or ideas in an informal and supportive setting
💡 20:00 Community Project Brainstorm for De Opening
🎶 20:30 Open Jam Session with live music and visuals

Bring your laptop, your curiosity, and your unfinished ideas. No experience required: everyone is welcome!

📍 Hosted by Baltan Laboratories at BlaBlaLab, Eindhoven
