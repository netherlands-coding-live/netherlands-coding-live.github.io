**DIY NIGHT**

**Bring Your Own Drinks!**

Hello everyone! We have an open jam in Rotterdam on Sunday 19th (from 18.00) Zomerhofstraat 71 Rotterdam (rechterhoek/right corner)

This is an informal jam and not exclusively live coding, anyone doing creative work (image or sound) with the computer, no matter what software, feel welcome to join. We will have slots of 15/20 minutes per set (duos) all the way through to the end (around 22.00)

Please let us know if you can join us. The more we are the better! The event is very DIY. Free entrance and there is no bar so you can bring your own drinks!

It will be a test event which we hope to repeat regularly so your contribution, both art and your ideas is very much appreciated.
